P. Marty Kump

Composer • Electrical Engineer • Wellness Enthusiast

I'm a guitarist, composer, and electrical engineer with a taste for variety and precision. My work spans genres, tempos, and tones—each piece aiming to be as interesting as it is well-crafted. Whether I'm building a sound or a circuit, I care about the details.

I won Steve Vai's "Shred the Web" contest and got to play live on stage with him—an unforgettable experience. I also placed runner-up in a nationwide contest judged by Judas Priest. I lived in New York City for over a decade, and now—less glamorously—I'm based in Kansas.
